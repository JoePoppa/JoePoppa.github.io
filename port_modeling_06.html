Modeling & Animation

HSS Jumbotron Spot

In the fall of 2010, USA Today Sports, HighSchoolSports.net put on a Super 25 - Massey Challenge game between two of the best ranked High Schools from the previous year, and other top regional teams.

This triple header event was broadcast on Fox Sports, with approximately 10,000 people in the live audience. My job was to help with marketing and promotion during the event from providing the on-field graphics, to banner graphics, to the 14x14 foot Jumbotron video that you see to the left. I used a few seconds of motion to draw the eye, then a loud sound (lightning) to draw the focus of the fans. Then we exposed them to our branding, and then we presented the event information, building up to exciting highlight reels on each team, and fishing with a 3D rendering of the actual trophy.

The video was a big hit at the event.
