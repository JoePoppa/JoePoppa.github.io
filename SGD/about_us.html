About Us

SG&D Communications and Design is a multi-faceted creative company of proven, highly-experienced and collaborative professionals—founded and led by Donna P. Barger—specializing in strategic planning, creative consulting, graphic design, interactive media, copy writing and production. We passionately help clarify our client's mission and goals – and then creative strategies to successfully achieve objectives. We are extremely detail oriented. The goal is to develop an appealing brand of visual style with the appropriate message and collateral.

 

My colleagues and I believe that every business has a great service or product that deserves recognition. We listen. We ask the questions. We get to the heart of the matter - and then take action to develop the right creative solutions for the right target markets.

 

Having a depth of experience in a variety of challenging situations enables us to determine quickly what the direction should be and what course of action should be taken.


SG&D can help you build a foundation or that special project that works to "Make Your Mark".

 

Making Your Mark: What does it mean to be "on our mark"?

For more than 28 years, SG&D Communications & Design has provided conceptual and targeted approaches to fulfill the marketing needs for the company’s client base, which includes local, regional and national small- to mid-sized organizations on time and within budget.

 

Mission

SG&D provides integrated creative solutions for businesses seeking marketing excellence.

 

Philosophy

The objective of our business is to not only strengthen brands, but strengthen future opportunities. We strive to enable your organization to make its mark effectively to grow your business. We truly take an active role in helping our clients succeed. Our mission, basic beliefs, goals and code of ethics are all summed up to more than two decades of successful client relationships.

 

Approach

We employ a wide network of highly skilled specialists to ensure that we have the most fitting skills available working on your behalf. No one person can maintain expertise in all areas of graphic design, advertising, marketing, interactive and print. Although you will be assigned to your own Account Manager, you will reap the benefits of an entire team of creative experts. Depending upon the specifics of the scope of work, our experienced and professional SG&D team is ready to assist you.
