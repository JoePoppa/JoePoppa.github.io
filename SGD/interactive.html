Interactive Design

Web

Websites have really evolved over the last ten years or so.  First, it was the daunting task of just “getting something up” on the world wide web because no one saw it as all that important. Then everyone graduated to an amazing amount of Flash, JQuery, games, mobile sites, apps, music and layers to attract attention.

 

Today, we are so inundated with constant and unending e-everything - that time and ease is of the essence!

 

Our goal is to design and development a website that will drive your brand in a clear, concise and user-friendly way so your target audience will locate your information quickly and without all the fuss.  

 

Since your products, services and other information are constantly changing, we will provide content management, search engine optimization, RSS feeds and ecommerce services to keep you fresh and current. 

 

Social Media

Social Media is one of the newer, and somewhat misunderstood, interactive tools to hit the marketing scene in recent years. Many people use social media for personal communications, but are unsure about the legitimacy of effectively implementing it into their businesses… but we do!

 

We can create your professional Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and Blog pages to reflect your company brand and have it correspond along with your website to give you the edge in communicating more effectively with your target audiences… but with a little more personality!
