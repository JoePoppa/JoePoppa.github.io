Who We Are.

SG&D Communications and Design (SG&D) is headquartered in the airport area of Pittsburgh, PA and has been providing a wide array of creative services to businesses in and around the greater Pittsburgh area and southwestern Pennsylvania since 1983.

SG&D specializes in corporate branding, website and mobile site design, social media, print design, public relations, media, strategic planning and creative consultation. We provide everything you need to professionally market your company and its products and services.

 

 

On Our Mark.

SG&D has provided conceptual and targeted approaches to fulfill the marketing needs for the company’s client base, which includes local, regional and national small- to mid-sized organizations on time and within budget.

 

Our award-winning comprehensive solutions will save you time, and we help you avoid costly mistakes by making the right choices the first time around.

 

Let us help you to make your mark and expand your reach today!

 

Idea. Vision. Reality.

Design helps mold ideas. It brings them to life - from an intangible to a tangible solution. At SG&D, we tap our creativity, knowledge, and experience to get to the heart of a client's idea. We transform that idea into a reality.

 

The reality can take many forms - from a brand identity, website or brochure to a trade show booth, ad or 3D animation - providing it fits the objective of our client's vision. It is a collaborative approach by a team of consummate professionals - a formula that has earned SG&D award-winning accolades over 30 years - that "makes your mark" come to life.
