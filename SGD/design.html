Design

Design is essentially problem solving and story telling. What story do you want to tell? In what ways do you want that story to reach the audience? Who is the audience? Do you want them to smile, laugh or feel a connection to your product or service? Does your brand have a professional look and feel?

 

A good designer must be able to understand your story and efficiently communicate it through style, animation, interactivity, video, photography, typography, color, and layout on the web, in print, or mobile.

 

Understanding Web Design

From a basic web presence to ecommerce, we understand how to design within the technology available. Our designs are effective and efficient, using the latest methods to style your site and make it an available and engaging experience to the most possible users.

 

Designing a mobile site or an email template hold similar challenges but are closely related to web design, but, we understand the differences in technology and know how to adapt our designs appropriately.

 

Understanding Print Design

Print still plays a very important role in our interactive world. It is a powerful, tactile communications tool that will tell your story, sell your product or service, and provide your target audience with a tangible impression that can be reference and used to take action.

 

Whether it’s a stationery package, brochure, catalog, direct mail, annual report, booth components, banners, newsletter, invitation or information package, SG&D will design an effective print piece to enhance and complete your cross-marketing efforts.
