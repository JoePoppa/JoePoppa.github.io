Planning

The first step in developing a successful brand is to establish your organization’s plan.

 

  • Executive Summary
  • Vision, Mission, Philosophy and Values
  • Goals and Objectives
  • Financial Plan to Support the Goals
  • Target Markets

 

Once your plan is developed, SG&D will create the best approach to increase
your visibility and awareness, and will provide on-going creative consultation and service to professionally implement the brand campaign.

 

Cross-marketing  

By implementing cross-marketing opportunities, SG&D will build a successful brand.

 

  • Corporate Identity

  • Website Design and Development

  • Blog and Social Media
