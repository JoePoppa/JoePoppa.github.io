Interface Design

Wingspan

Working with a multiple time former colleague and friend, Michael Wheatley, we've redesigned and rebuilt Wingspan's website from the ground up with new functionality, product icons, design and styling.

It's built with WordPress on request to make it easy for non HTML oriented content managers to add new content.

To keep it iPad and iPhone friendly, we used jQuery effects on the rotating carousel rather than Adobe Flash.

The style of the site is designed to be pharmaceutical friendly, with sharp, crisp blues and whites, sitting over a stylized stainless steel surface. The images are bold, colorful, and as close as possible.
