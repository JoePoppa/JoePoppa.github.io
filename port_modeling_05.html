Modeling & Animation

Fisher Scientific - Seymour

Fisher Scientific had approved a slick little drawing of a scientist character to promote their "Blue" line of products, but, the flat illustration couldn't do the many things the internal customer (Research Division) wanted to do with the character.

So, I built Seymour in 3D.

He's been featured in at least two flash games that I was project lead on, (clean your lab & soccer) as well as promotion for a mini-site, large promotional hero spots, giveaways, print materials and a video for the PittConn trade show which I'd storyboarded, animated and helped to finalize.

Seymour has been used on almost everything, and I've done some projects with the flat, 2D version of Seymour as well.
