GSK - Grab'n'Go Lab

Glaxo Smith Klien approached the Fisher Scientific Marketing and Communications group for a new mascot for their new Grab'n'Go carts.

People are partial to their pets, and, the customer wanted his White English Lab, "Blue" to be immortalized as a 3D Mascot for the product.

With limited time and budget, I created this posable, personable 3D model of his happy ol' dog.

The model has morph states to quickly chang expression, and move his ears. The safety glasses and vest are easily hidden, but stay attached to the physique to be easily added or removed, no matter what the pose.

They'd asked for the mascot to be catching a frisbee and retrieving a box of FisherBrand product from the cart... so there ya go.
