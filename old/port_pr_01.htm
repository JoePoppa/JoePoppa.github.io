PNC Bank has requested a wide variety of marketing posters over the years. This piece was part of a strategy for them to encourage a positive mind set for PNC Bank employees.

The heart logo in the center was something I'd developed earlier on for the client. They had asked for a logo that would be loose and friendly, to print onto plaques, posters, mouse pads and more. I provided several options, and they chose the logo in the center of this poster. The design of the logo then prompted the design of the poster itself.

This poster was printed out 2'x3'. Even the script font text was legible at quite a distance.
