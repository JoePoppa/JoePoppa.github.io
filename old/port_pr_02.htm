This 22" x 11" poster is one of four developed for Pearson Education so they could market their new Security training internally across the company. After Pearson Education chose the adventure island theme from among many proposed, I worked with them to have sketches approved and develop this 3D Island Game board. They wanted a warm, positive association for this courseware. Incorporating the renderings of this custom 3D Model into large posters was just one of the many ways this asset was reused.
